wonderla, Hyderabad :

Wonderla Hyderabad Amusement & Water Park is undoubtedly a thrilling venue of unlimited fun and unparalleled entertainment for visitors looking for an adrenalin rush and memorable experience. It offers international standards of facilities along with a wide array of exciting dry and water rides, stimulating activities, and the host of awe-inspiring games. There are almost 43 different rides, varying from high thrill rides, to family rides and special kid rides. There are many eatery joints which offer delectable food to pamper your taste buds. You can opt for a luxurious night stay or a fun-filled day tour for a stimulating experience.
